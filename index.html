21 Million Race - Free Bitcoin Arcade Game

Help Michael Saylor from Strategy on his journey across Digital Manhattan! Welcome to 21 Million Race, the ultimate Bitcoin-themed arcade platformer game. In this action-packed browser game, you play as the legendary Bitcoin advocate on a mission to stack sats and defeat fiat currency enemies.

Game Features

  • Iconic laser eyes power-up inspired by Bitcoin Twitter culture
  • Battle against fiat villains trying to stop your Bitcoin stack
  • Collect satoshis and race to accumulate 21 million
  • Retro pixel art graphics with smooth platformer controls
  • Original Bitcoin-themed soundtrack by @lilbubble
  • Free to play directly in your web browser
  • Works on desktop and mobile devices

How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move and jump. Press E to activate laser eyes. On mobile, use the on-screen controls. Double-tap up to jump higher. Avoid enemies or defeat them with your laser eyes while collecting as many satoshis as possible.

About Bitcoin and 21 Million

The game's name references Bitcoin's fixed supply cap of 21 million coins, making it the hardest money ever created. Join the race to stack sats before the supply runs out!
