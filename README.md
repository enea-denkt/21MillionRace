# 21 Million Race - The Bitcoin Arcade Game

![21 Million Race](assets/Game.png)

Help Strategy's Michael Saylor on his journey across Digital Manhattan! A free Bitcoin-themed arcade platformer where you battle altcoins and Short Jim, collect sats, and race to stack 21 million.

## Play Now

Click [here](https://enea-denkt.github.io/21MillionRace/) in your browser to play.

## Features

- **Laser Eyes** - Press E to activate laser eyes and defeat enemies
- **Battle Altcoins & Wall Street Villains** - Fight against altcoins and Short Jim trying to stop your Bitcoin stack
- **Collect Satoshis** - Race to accumulate 21 million sats
- **Retro Pixel Art** - Classic arcade-style graphics with smooth platformer controls
- **Original Soundtrack** - Bitcoin-themed music by [@lilbubble](https://x.com/lilbubble)
- **Cross-Platform** - Works on desktop and mobile devices

## Controls

### Desktop
- **Arrow Keys / WASD** - Move and jump
- **E** - Laser eyes
- **Double-tap Up** - Jump higher

### Mobile
- **D-Pad** - Movement controls
- **Action Button** - Laser eyes

## About

The game's name references Bitcoin's fixed supply cap of 21 million coins - the hardest money ever created. In a world where fiat currencies are constantly debased, you step into the shoes of Michael Saylor and embark on an epic journey across Digital Manhattan to stack as many satoshis as possible before they're all gone.

Battle against altcoin crypto villains and the Short Jim who want to stop your Bitcoin accumulation. Use your laser eyes (the ultimate symbol of Bitcoin conviction) to defeat enemies and protect your stack.

Whether you're a seasoned Bitcoiner or just discovering the world of sound money, 21 Million Race brings the excitement of the Bitcoin revolution to life in a fun, retro arcade experience. Stack sats, have fun, and remember: there will only ever be 21 million.
